Masumiyat - as the name suggests, is a philanthropic group registered as a nonprofit organisation ,with an aim to aid the underprivileged and neglected sections of the society to realise their dream of living a respectful and dignified life by catering their basic needs of education and health.

we are being three-dimensional

Masumiyat is a growing organisation, began as an endeavour of an endeavour of 4 college mates. It is the brainchild of the youths, all coming from decent families who are a firm believer that giving is a way of life and are committed to serve the society. Our actions focus on following essential pillars:

Health and hygiene
Quality education
environment
awareness about human rights

Under each pillar of substantial development, different campaigns are conducted by a team of self-motivated, hard-working individuals, working diligently to achieve the dream of a 'social egalitarian' society possible.

Our aim is to liberate hapless people from all existing barriers and facilitate their ways towards the zenith. We'll be glad to shake hands with like-minded people, possessing the sole purpose of social welfare.

EDUCATION

Education is not the answer to all the questions, instead it is the means to answer all the questions. Hence, here we are the Masumiyat foundation to answer all the questions of the lives of poor children by providing them with education. Masumiyat foundation is a NGO in India which has proved to be very fruitful to many proverty stricken lives of children and still plan to continue further in future too. Our NGO has worked on reaching to the underprivileged children of the society to give them education. We have provided the children with all the necessary materials for their studies. Also counselled them for their careers in respective fields according to their interests so that they with education can lift up themselves and their families from the filth of poverty for a better life.

HEALTH

We at Masumiyat Foundation are commited towards raising health standards of our society. We have organised various health and blood donation camps in various parts of Delhi. The first step towards change is awareness. We have conducted various workshops educating people about healthy habits. Also we know that Malaria and Dengue are easily spreading vector borne diseases. Making India malaria and dengue free is our goal. We are working closely on society hygiene to remove such diseases caused due to pollution , lack of awareness and poor health standards. Through all these activities we are working against health hazards faced by the people right from the childhod to old age. Every person has the RIGHT TO LIVE in a healthy atmosphere. We are organising blood donation camps as it acts a link between voluntary donors with those who need blood.
